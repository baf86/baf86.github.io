What is By the People?
By the People is a free, city-wide international arts and dialogue festival presented by Halcyon, which supports innovative emerging creatives through fellowships in art and social entrepreneurship. Since its debut in 2018, By The People has presented free and accessible art and performance to over a two dozen venues in all four quadrants of Washington, DC through the help of our community partners. Curatorial Vision 2020: The preliminary thematic framework for By the People 2020 is entitled CULTURE SUPREME featuring installations and performances addressing the current shift in how Americans enact and shape democracy, from a political to a cultural perspective. Artwork at BTP 2020 will examine how culture is the single driving force in our major societal systems. CULTURE SUPREME will include both local and nationally-acclaimed artists who will present multi-disciplinary works which ask us to consider the society in which we live, the systems and institutions we take for granted, and dialogues around the future we want to build for humanity.

Who is eligible to apply?
Artists working in any discipline who can commit to creating and exhibiting work during June 2020 (key dates listed below). Applications can be submitted by single artists or artist collectives that meet the following criteria:
  • Have demonstrated experience developing and executing works or projects, especially in public spaces.
  • Artists must be over 21 years of age, and are eligible to work in the United States. International applicants are welcome to apply!

How will the artists be selected?
By The People 2020 Curator and members of the By The People team will review applications on a rolling basis. Shortlisted finalists will be invited to interview with the team and submit a more detailed proposal including in-depth concept outline, site parameters (which will be discussed upon acceptance to interview round), production timeline, sketches of proposed project, and budget.
