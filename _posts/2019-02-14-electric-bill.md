You get a bill every two months here in Merida, Mexico. 169 pesos = $8.72
My air conditioner is on every day here except maybe three weeks out of the year, if that! I enjoy my every day dress weather and looking just as sexy as can be, but it can take its toll. Nevertheless I'm NOT complaining.
